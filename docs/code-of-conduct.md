# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Sales Wiki is an open, welcoming community for sales, RevOps, and finance professionals. We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful**: Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Be constructive**: Focus on improving content, not criticizing people
- **Assume good intent**: Most disagreements stem from different contexts
- **Stay on topic**: Keep discussions relevant to sales operations
- **Cite sources**: Back up claims with data or experience when possible

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information
- Spam or promotional content
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. Warning from maintainers
2. Temporary ban from contributing
3. Permanent ban for severe or repeated violations

Report violations by emailing [conduct@sales-wiki.org](mailto:conduct@sales-wiki.org) or opening a private issue.
