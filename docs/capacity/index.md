# Capacity

## What is the Capacity Section?

The **Capacity section** covers **states, amounts, and flows** of selling capacity within your sales organization.

### Focus: "How much capacity do we have/need/lost?"

**Nature:** Absolute amounts, gaps, and changes over time

**What it measures:** Selling capacity measured in dollars or RRE (Ramped Rep Equivalent)

### Examples:

- **Current Capacity:** $7.0M - How much selling capacity you have right now
- **Gap to Target:** $3.0M shortfall - The difference between where you are and where you need to be
- **Recovered Gap:** $0.9M regained - Capacity you've recovered through backfills
- **Unrecovered Gap:** $4.1M still lost - Capacity that remains unrecovered
- **Loss Capacity:** $5.0M lost to attrition - Total capacity lost to terminations and leaves
