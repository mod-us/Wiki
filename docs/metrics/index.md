# Metrics

## What is the Metrics Section?

The **Metrics section** covers **measurements, ratios, and performance indicators** that help you quantify and track sales performance and efficiency.

### Focus: "How do we calculate and benchmark?"

**Nature:** Often ratios, percentages, or conversion formulas

**What it measures:** Performance, efficiency, and operational KPIs

### Examples:

- **RRE:** Converts headcount â†’ capacity equivalents (5 reps at various ramp stages = 3.5 RRE)
- **Attrition Rate:** 45.5% - Percentage of capacity lost to attrition (lost capacity / beginning capacity)
- **Sales Efficiency Ratio:** Revenue growth / S&M spend - How efficiently you're deploying capital
- **Backfill Rate:** 81% - Percentage of lost capacity recovered through backfills
- **Recovery Lag:** 4.5 months - Average time from termination to full capacity restoration
