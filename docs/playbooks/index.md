# Playbooks

## What is the Playbooks Section?

The **Playbooks section** provides **step-by-step guides and best practices** for executing common sales operations processes.

### Focus: "How do we actually do this?"

**What it covers:** Practical guides, processes, templates, and workflows for sales capacity and performance management

### Why Use Playbooks?

Playbooks translate concepts and metrics into action:

- **Capacity Planning:** How to build a quarterly capacity plan
- **Backfill Planning:** When and how to replace departed employees
- **Territory Planning:** How to design and assign sales territories
- **Hiring Planning:** How to build hiring timelines with ramp schedules
- **Attrition Management:** How to track and respond to turnover

These are the operational guides you'll reference when actually doing the work.
